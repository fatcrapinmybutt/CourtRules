## Summary: Key Deadlines at a Glance

| Event | Deadline |
|---|---|
| Claim of appeal (civil) from circuit court to COA | 21 days from entry of order |
| Application for leave to appeal to COA | 21 days (delayed: 12 months) |
| Appellant's brief (COA) | 56 days after claim of appeal filed |
| Appellee's brief (COA) | 35 days after appellant's brief served |
| Reply brief (COA) | 21 days after appellee's brief served |
| Motion for reconsideration (COA) | 21 days from opinion |
| Application for leave to appeal to Supreme Court | 56 days from COA decision |
| Delayed application to Supreme Court | Within 6 months, with good cause |
| Bypass application to Supreme Court | 42 days from circuit court judgment |
| Cross-application to Supreme Court | 21 days from original application |
