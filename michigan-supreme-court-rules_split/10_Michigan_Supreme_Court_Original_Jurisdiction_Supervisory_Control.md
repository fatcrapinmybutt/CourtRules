## Michigan Supreme Court — Original Jurisdiction & Supervisory Control

The Michigan Supreme Court exercises:
- **Original jurisdiction** in habeas corpus, mandamus, prohibition, quo warranto, and certiorari (Const 1963, Art 6, § 4)
- **Superintending control** over all courts (MCR 7.301(A)(2))
- **Supervisory power** over attorney discipline (MCR 9.117)

### Appeals to Michigan Supreme Court

- Applications for leave to appeal must be filed within:
  - **56 days** from COA judgment in civil cases (MCR 7.305(C)(2)(a))
  - **56 days** from COA judgment in criminal cases (MCR 7.305(C)(2)(b))
- Bypass applications from circuit court — within **42 days**
- Peremptory reversals available without full briefing
- Cross-appeals governed by MCR 7.305(C)(3)
